We are The Astrocoders, a group of curious young innovators participating in NASA's Space Apps Challenge. United by our passion for space, science, and technology, we aim to make the wonders of the universe more accessible. Through our project, we designed an interactive app where users can explore 3D models of planets, rotate them with a touch, and experience space in a new, engaging way.
